Miriam Kuzbary

I am originally from Garland, Texas, and my family is from Damascus, Syria, and various places in Texas, Oklahoma, and Tennessee. I live in Atlanta, Georgia with my spouse and our two cats. Before the pandemic, when I was not doing math I was rock climbing regularly and occasionally performing in a band. Now, in my free time I play oud and guitar in my apartment.

I am very interested in the preservation and continuation of Syrian traditional music and art, and am casually interested about the mathematics underlying the music and art of the region.
