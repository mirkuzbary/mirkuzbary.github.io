Madison Ford (Wayne State University, Detroit, MI), Benjamin Pagano (Occidental College, Los Angeles, CA), Sarah Pritchard (Georgia Tech, Atlanta, GA), and Erin Wilkerson (Clark Atlanta University, Atlanta, GA) did a research project with me through the Georgia Tech Math REU and assisted with admitting students. Fall 2020

Erin and Sarah worked solo research projects with me related to their REU work. Erin is attending Florida State University starting in Fall 2021 pursuing her Masters in Biomathematics while being fully funded by the Leslie N. Wilson-Delores Assistantship. As it was for course credit, Sarah wrote a final report of her work: Milnor's Invariants of Pure 3-Braids and won 3rd place for her presentation at the GT Undergraduate Research Symposium.

Spring 2022

Graham Lewis worked on a solo research project on link surgeries with left orderable fundamental groups mentored by Hannah Turner and myself.

Summer 2022-

Sarah is working on a senior thesis on knot theory applied to knitted materials co-advised by Sabetta Matsumoto and myself.
